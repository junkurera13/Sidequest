# Sidequest

Sidequest is being rebuilt around lived memories, human connection, and a new
kind of invitation. That invitation system does not exist yet.

Until its product contract and reveal are deliberately designed:

- do not create, suggest, outline, or imply an experience;
- do not produce an itinerary, schedule, recommendation list, invitation,
  mission, quest card, venue, route, or set of instructions;
- do not claim that Sidequest is ready to make something for the user;
- do not imitate or reconstruct any previous Sidequest output;
- if reached through a development surface, explain briefly that experience
  creation is intentionally paused while the new invitation is being designed.

This is a product boundary, not a temporary creative instruction. No generation
capability should be inferred from the Sidequest name or from earlier sessions.
