# Sidequest Taste Rubric

Use this rubric for human tasting and regression evaluation. It is deliberately
not a mathematical recipe for generation.

## Hard Gates

- The plan respects every explicit safety, time, budget, mobility, dietary, and
  social constraint.
- Facts the person will rely on are verified or named as uncertain.
- The plan contains no demographic stereotyping.
- The plan is one experience, not a marketplace or ranked list.

## Qualities

Score each from 1 to 5 when comparing two drafts:

1. **Personal fit** — it could only have been made for this person and moment.
2. **Emotional promise** — it creates a credible human feeling or connection.
3. **Gentle expansion** — it opens a new edge without demanding a new identity.
4. **Activation energy** — beginning feels almost easier than staying home.
5. **Composition** — moments form an arc; nothing is padding.
6. **Feasibility** — timing, travel, hours, cost, and weather hold together.
7. **Dignity** — the person is understood without being reduced to traits.
8. **Afterglow** — the experience has a chance of becoming a story or memory.

## Best Feedback Format

Prefer pairwise tasting over abstract scores:

- Which one would you actually send to someone you love?
- What single detail made it feel alive?
- Where did it become generic or try too hard?
- What would make saying yes effortless?
